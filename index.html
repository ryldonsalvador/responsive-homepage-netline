Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need new content to use this?

Nope! Run your campaign just like you always do and add qualifying questions directly to your registration form. No new content. No added build. No disruption.

How fast can I launch?

Fast. With no dev work, HQL Precision can be live in minutes—not months.

What can I ask my buyers?

Whatever tells you they're serious. From pain points to investment timelines, you choose the questions and the disqualifiers.

How does this help my sales team?

Every lead comes with built-in buyer context so reps know what to say, when to say it, and why the buyer might care. No more “who is this?” follow-ups.
